# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system, based on the synchronized theme.

## 1. Theme Overview

Our design system utilizes a **dark** color mode for its primary interface, providing a modern and comfortable viewing experience, especially in low-light environments.

## 2. Typography

The typographic scale is designed for optimal readability and brand consistency across all platforms.

*   **Headlines:** Crafted with the `Manrope` typeface, headlines are designed to be impactful and draw attention, reflecting a contemporary and legible aesthetic.
*   **Body Text:** All body copy employs the `Inter` typeface, chosen for its versatility, readability at various sizes, and clean lines, ensuring clear communication of information.
*   **Labels:** Interactive elements and labels also utilize the `Inter` typeface, maintaining consistency and clarity for user interactions.

## 3. Color Palette

Our color palette is built around a vibrant and engaging set of colors, ensuring visual appeal and functional distinction.

*   **Primary Color:** The foundational brand color is `#00C853`, a bright and energetic green that signifies success, growth, and interaction. It is used for key calls to action and primary interactive elements.
*   **Secondary Color:** Supporting the primary, `#FFD700` (a golden yellow) serves as a complementary accent, suitable for secondary actions, highlight elements, or informational tags.
*   **Tertiary Color:** An additional accent, `#00E5FF` (a vivid cyan), adds another layer of vibrancy and can be used for badges, special indicators, or decorative touches.
*   **Neutral Color:** The primary neutral color for backgrounds and surfaces is `#0066ff`. This deep blue provides a strong contrast for content and complements the vibrant palette.

## 4. Shape and Form

The geometric properties of our UI elements contribute significantly to the overall feel of the system.

*   **Roundedness:** A `roundedness` value of `2` indicates a moderate level of corner rounding, striking a balance between modern softness and structural clarity. This provides a friendly yet professional appearance.

## 5. Spacing

The spatial relationships between elements are carefully considered to enhance readability and user experience.

*   **Spacing:** A `spacing` value of `2` signifies a normal and balanced amount of whitespace. This ensures elements are distinct without feeling overly dense or sparse, promoting a comfortable visual flow.
